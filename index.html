About Me

I didn’t get into software just to build things — I got into it to forge myself. It’s a discipline that sharpens my mind and keeps me grounded. I didn’t take the conventional path. Most of what I know, I’ve learned by building real systems under real pressure — making mistakes, studying, and iterating with intention. I value clarity over cleverness, fundamentals over trends, and purpose over polish. This portfolio reflects that journey. It’s not just about what I’ve built, but how I’ve built — with focus, resolve, and a deep respect for the craft.
